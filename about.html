About the Chapter

Unity, Welfare, and Leadership

Who We Are

The Kenyan Students' Chapter in Gujarat is a student-led community that brings together Kenyan students studying across universities in Gujarat State, India. The Chapter exists to promote unity, mutual support, and shared growth among members.

Our Purpose

Community & Belonging

Creating a home away from home for Kenyan students.

Student Welfare

Supporting members through structured welfare systems and initiatives.

Leadership & Growth

Encouraging leadership, responsibility, and active participation.

What We Do

Orientation & Support

Helping new students settle in through guidance and assistance.

Events & Engagement

Organizing cultural, social, and national events.

Financial Welfare

Managing contributions, savings systems, and member support funds.
