Meetings & Discipline

Structure • Accountability • Commitment

Meeting Schedule

When

Last Sunday of every month

Time

5:00 PM

Grace Period

30 minutes (after which fines apply)

Attendance Rules

  • Members are expected to attend all meetings regularly
  • Absence must be communicated at least 1 day prior
  • Emergencies are considered exceptions
  • “Personal reasons” do not automatically exempt fines
  • A trusted member may privately request an excuse on your behalf

Fines & Penalties

Late Arrival

INR 100

Absenteeism

INR 200 (without valid reason)

Payment

Fines must be paid promptly to the Treasurer

Special Considerations

Members facing genuine challenges (such as long distance or unavoidable circumstances) may be granted flexibility upon committee approval.

Example: Members like Kurgat have previously been granted flexibility due to distance.

Respectful Communication

  • All communication must remain respectful and professional
  • Avoid offensive language or emotional outbursts
  • Constructive and open-ended discussions are encouraged

Commitment Builds Strong Communities

Regular attendance and discipline ensure the success of our Chapter.
