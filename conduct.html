Code of Conduct

Respectful Communication

  • Members must communicate professionally and respectfully
  • Avoid offensive language, personal attacks, or disruptive behavior
  • Open-ended responses are encouraged to address concerns constructively

Accountability & Responsibility

  • Members must fulfill financial obligations (kiti, merry-go-round) on time
  • Attend meetings regularly or inform the group of valid reasons in advance
  • Members are accountable for their actions in Chapter activities

Conflict Resolution

  • Conflicts should be resolved internally through discussion among members
  • Serious issues are escalated to the executive committee for mediation
  • Transparency, fairness, and confidentiality are upheld at all times

Ethics & Integrity

  • Members must act with honesty, integrity, and fairness
  • Misuse of welfare funds, falsifying records, or violating rules will lead to disciplinary action
  • Compliance with Chapter policies is mandatory
