About Our Chapter

A home base for Kenyan students studying in Gujarat.

The Kenyan Students' Chapter in Gujarat brings together students across universities to build a strong, supportive, and vibrant community.

We focus on welfare, leadership, accountability, cultural unity, and practical support for students navigating life and study away from home.
