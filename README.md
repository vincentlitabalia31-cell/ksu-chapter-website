🇰🇪 Kenyan Students Chapter – Gujarat Website

📌 Overview

This project is the official website for the Kenyan Students Chapter based in Gujarat, India. It serves as a central platform to provide information about the chapter, its leadership, membership structure, welfare initiatives, and community activities.

The website is designed to enhance communication, transparency, and engagement among members while also presenting a professional image to external audiences.

---

🎯 Objectives

- Provide clear information about the chapter and its operations
- Showcase leadership and organizational structure
- Support member welfare and communication
- Promote events, activities, and initiatives

---

🧩 Features

- 🏠 Home page with overview of the chapter
- 👥 Leadership section
- 📋 Membership information
- 💰 Welfare and contributions page
- 📄 Additional pages (conduct, activities, etc.)
- 🎨 Responsive and simple user interface

---

🛠️ Technologies Used

- HTML5
- CSS3
