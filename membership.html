Membership & Induction

Join • Contribute • Grow

Eligibility

  • Must be a Kenyan student enrolled in a university in Gujarat State
  • Must agree to abide by Chapter policies and code of conduct

Membership Contributions

Registration Fee

INR 500 (one-time)

Monthly Kiti

INR 500 per month

Merry-go-round

INR 1500 rotational contribution

Joining Process

1. Register

Fill the New Member Registration Form

2. Submit Details

Name, program, email, contact, arrival date

3. Confirmation

Await approval from Secretary and Treasurer

4. Orientation

Attend welcome and induction session

Member Responsibilities

  • Fulfill all financial obligations on time
  • Participate in Chapter activities and meetings
  • Maintain respectful communication with members

Exit / Withdrawal Policy

  • Members must clear all dues before exiting
  • Provide at least 2 months’ notice
  • Receive 80% of kiti share (adjusted for expenses)
